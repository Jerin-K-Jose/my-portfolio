Project Overview

The Smart Agriculture System (SmAS) is an IoT-based project designed to modernize traditional farming practices. Developed as a mini-project, it leverages real-time monitoring of soil moisture, temperature, and humidity to optimize irrigation systems.

Using NodeMCU ESP32 and various sensors, SmAS integrates seamlessly with the Blynk IoT cloud platform to provide farmers with actionable insights and control from their smartphones, ensuring efficient water usage and improved crop health.

Detailed Explanation

The Smart Agriculture System utilizes a combination of sensors, microcontrollers, and IoT technology to automate irrigation and monitor environmental conditions. Key features include:

  • DHT11 Sensor: Measures temperature and humidity to monitor the environmental conditions affecting crops.
  • Soil Moisture Sensor: Detects soil moisture levels and triggers automated irrigation when levels fall below a threshold.
  • NodeMCU ESP32: Acts as the central controller, collecting data from sensors and sending it to the Blynk IoT cloud.
  • Blynk IoT Integration: Allows users to monitor and control the system remotely via a smartphone app.

The system is designed to be energy-efficient and scalable, enabling its deployment in small farms and larger agricultural setups alike.
